CHOOSE YOUR UNDERTONE
STILL UNSURE HOW TO FIND YOUR UNDERTONE?
For Lighter Skin Tones:

Check your veins!
If your veins appear to be green or olive, you have a warm undertone
If your veins appear blue-green, you have a neutral skintone
If your veins appear to be bluish and/or purple, then you have a cool undertone
For Darker Skin Tones:

Stand in front of a white background
and determine which colors appear the most apparent in the skin
using the stark contrast with the white background to make that observation
